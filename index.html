Care Sense

What is Care Sense?

Care Sense is a non-wearable fall detection device that combines IoT technology with compassionate elderly care. Our system detects falls within 15 seconds and immediately alerts family members and caregivers through mobile applications and speaker notifications. Unlike traditional wearable solutions, Care Sense requires no devices to be worn, making it the perfect choice for active seniors who prioritize comfort and independence.
